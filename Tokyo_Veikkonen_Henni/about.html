When Christmas draws near, Tokyo is filled with amazing illuminations and many festive decorations. See the popular lights at Caretta Shiodome in central Tokyo or in Shinjuku in attition to many others.

Spring

During the Spring time the whole city turns pink when the sakura, the cherryblossoms, bloom. This time is very short so it is traditionally cherished by having a picnic and flowe-viewing. A great place to enjoy the flowers is the Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden,Yoyogi and Inokashira parks.

Summer
